# Mission

sophiamcconnell.com is primarily a personal project. It exists for Sophia first, and for anyone else who finds it second.

## Core Purposes

1. **Writing to think**: A place to publish essays and thoughts. Writing is how Sophia processes ideas — this site gives that writing somewhere to live.
2. **Experimentation**: A sandbox for practicing Claude Code and trying out development ideas in a real, low-stakes context.
3. **A pointer**: Something to send to people when they ask — a fuller picture than a LinkedIn profile, without the performance of a personal brand.

## Aesthetic

Indie-web and nature-themed. The site should feel handmade and a little unconventional — not polished or corporate. The unstructured, evolving quality of it is part of the charm, not a problem to fix.

## Audience

Sophia herself, mostly. Occasionally someone she's pointed here. The site is not built for traffic or discovery.

## Tone

Personal and direct. The voice here is how Sophia actually thinks and talks — which, as she'd note, is in its essence a personal brand, just an honest one.

## What It Is Not

- A platform built for an audience
- A professional services or consulting site
- A social media replacement
- Something optimized for SEO or discoverability
